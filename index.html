About Froggy

How Does Froggy Say GM?

Are you familiar with someone who sounds like a two-stroke engine or even sings like a frog? Before 1997, nobody had a clue. That year, 17-year-old Swede Daniel Malmedahl became known as the 'Two-Stroke Human Combustion Engine.' Just when many thought the craze was over, in 2003, Erik Wernquist created the 'Two-Stroked Combustion Engine Frog' amidst the snows. Fast forward to 2023, celebrating history and memes, we now have the 'Crazy Two-Stroke Solana Frog' - a wild addition to the saga. This new phenomenon is aptly nicknamed 'Short-Crazy Frog'
